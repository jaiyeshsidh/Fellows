Introduction

About IncubateIND Fellowship

INCIND Technology Fellowship is one of its kind, exclusive program for aspiring developers, coders, hackers, designers and innovators to develop their skills, and work/intern with senior technology leaders and industry experts.

IncubateIND over the years have worked with lot of students who despite being very talented are looking at opportunities to sharpen their skills and work with Industry leaders to understand the complexities of technology world. The Fellowship is an initiative that will help students to come in direct touch with technology leaders. The fellowship will also improve the chances of the selected students to get selected and hired by their dream organizations.

For You

Benifits for being a Fellows

  • Interaction: Interaction with top technology leaders globally
  • Exposure: A chance to participate and publicize various technology events and be the thought leader in technological events domain.
  • Networking: IncubateIND events participation will ensure that you get to hob nob with senior technology leaders and build your relationships with them for better future.
  • Certification and Goodies: Get certifications and recommendations letters that would enhance your resume highlighting the efforts you have done with IncubateIND.
  • Internship with technology startups; team IncubateIND will help facilitate this

For You

Responsibilities as being a Fellows

  • Hosting a networking event every month in your college, and the speaker will be recommended by us.
  • Conduct workshops and seminars in your college
  • Organize and manage INCUBATEIND presence in college fests and other technical events.
  • Involve students from first to final year to actively participate in INCUBATEIND activities and contributions. Contributions can be manifold like:
    1. Articles
    2. Technical blogs
    3. Projects
    4. New programming problems
    5. Interview experiences
    6. Videos
    7. InfoGraphics
  • Internship with technology startups; team IncubateIND will help facilitate this
